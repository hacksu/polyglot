09 - Why It Matters

Why Does This Matter?

Security & CTF

  • CTF challenges hide flags in polyglot files
  • PDF+JS polyglots used in real malware
  • GIFAR bypassed same-origin policy (CVE patched)
  • JPEG+PHP bypasses file upload filters
  • PHAR archives used in PHP deserialization attacks

Parser Literacy

  • Forces deep understanding of grammar rules
  • Exposes hidden assumptions in specs
  • Great compiler/interpreter exercise
  • Reveals why # means different things in C vs Python
  • Builds intuition for format validation bugs

Art & Creativity

  • Quines - programs that print themselves
  • Multi-language quines in competitions
  • Code golf & esoteric programming
  • Images that are also runnable programs
  • The IOCCC (Obfuscated C Contest)

Famous real-world polyglots

  • PHAR polyglots - PHP archive upload bypasses
  • PDF+ZIP - standard CTF forensics puzzle
  • MP4+ZIP - video files that unzip cleanly
  • Polyglot quines - self-printing in 5+ languages
